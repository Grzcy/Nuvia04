    Grazzy Community Guidelines

    Welcome to Grazzy — a creative, inclusive community built on respect, safety, and authenticity. By using Grazzy, you agree to follow these rules so everyone can enjoy a positive experience. Repeated or severe violations may lead to content removal, feature restrictions, suspension, or permanent bans.

    Core Principles

    • Be respectful

      No harassment, hate, bullying, or targeted attacks. Disagree with ideas, not people. Slurs and dehumanizing content are not allowed.

    • Keep everyone safe

      No threats or glorification of violence, self-harm encouragement, or dangerous acts. Report risks immediately.

    • Protect privacy

      No doxxing or sharing private information (addresses, IDs, private chats, photos) without express consent.

    Content Rules

    • Inappropriate or explicit content

      No pornographic content, sexual exploitation, graphic gore, or sexual content involving minors. Mature topics must remain non-graphic and context-appropriate.

    • Spam and platform abuse

      No mass-tagging, deceptive links, repetitive promos, or bot-driven posting that disrupts conversations or feeds.

    • Intellectual property

      Only share content you own or have rights to. We remove infringing content and may act on repeat violations.

    Security & Integrity

    • Authenticity

      No impersonation of users, creators, or Grazzy staff. Don’t create accounts to evade bans or manipulate engagement.

    • Technical misuse

      No exploits, malware, scraping that harms service, or unauthorized automation that interferes with normal operation.

    • Illegal activity

      Don’t use Grazzy to promote, coordinate, or transact illegal goods or services. We cooperate with law enforcement when required.

    Platform-Specific

    • Groups & chats

      Follow group-specific rules. Keep topics on track. Refrain from trolling, brigading, or derailing discussions.

    • DMs

      Unwanted advances, spam, or harassment in private messages are treated the same as public violations. Block and report when needed.

    • Levels, JCoins, and rewards

      Don’t game the system with fake engagement or coordinated manipulation. Violations may lead to reversal of rewards or suspensions.

    Reporting & Enforcement

    • Report violations

      Use the Report button on content or profiles. Provide clear context or screenshots when possible so we can respond faster.

    • Fair process

      Moderation considers severity, intent, and history. We may warn, restrict, or remove accounts to protect the community.

    • Appeals

      If you believe a decision was incorrect, submit an appeal from Settings. Don’t debate actions publicly; it disrupts others.

    Need to report a violation?

    Reports are confidential. Thank you for helping keep Grazzy safe.

    A reminder for creators

    • Use appropriate language

      Keep conversations broadly welcoming. Strong language or sensitive topics must remain non-abusive and contextual.

    • Zero tolerance for child exploitation

      Any content or behavior that harms or sexualizes minors results in immediate removal and reporting to authorities.

    • Keep your account secure

      Never share passwords or sensitive info. Grazzy staff will never ask for your password in DMs.
